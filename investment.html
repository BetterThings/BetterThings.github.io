Investment Services

“In the long run, it’s not just how much money you make that will determine your future prosperity. It’s how much of that money you put to work by saving it and investing it.” - Peter Lynch

Proper Asset Allocation

Correctly done, asset allocation is a risk reduction strategy and is the single most important decision relating to the portfolio. It is also a boring, no-nonsense, businesslike approach to portfolio management. We create asset allocation strategies based on investors’ risk and return objectives and time horizons.

Simply diversifying into many different investments is not going to increase risk adjusted returns over the long term. We ensure that portfolios consist of a number of different classes of investments which are not highly positively correlated to one another and that portfolios are periodically rebalanced.

Controlling Costs

As we develop the optimal investment strategy for each portfolio, it is imperative that we never lose sight of the costs of your investments, so that more of your money goes to work for you. We believe that limiting your investment costs are a key factor in creating successful, long-term results.

Since all investments have costs; our job is to take necessary steps to ensure that your portfolio works as efficiently as possible. You will never pay a front-end loaded fund commission with us and we do not benefit financially from generating more transactions in your account as a broker might.

Prudence and Disclipline

Contrary to the opinions of individuals and brokers prone to try to “pick the winners”, we realize that what the markets do is not within our control – so we focus on wealth management factors that are within our control such as patiently building a properly diversified portfolio of cost efficient investments.
