Charles "Chip" Estes
President Better Things Financial Partners, LLC
Mission While working in the insurance, banking and investment industry over the last 10 years, I became convinced there is a better way for people to receive financial or retirement planning and investment services. Clients too often feel caught between Wall Street Broker-Dealers with their excessive fees and commissions, or having to manage their savings and investments themselves.
I also found that many clients were making significant decisions about asset allocation, retirement income production, social security timing and benefits without proper planning. Mistakes made now now can have serious consequences in the future.
Because I'm analytical, I enjoy looking at as many financial products and service providers as possible. Since my goal has always been to find the best financial and retirement solutions for my clients, I will continue to evaluate the broadest selection of products available.
Thus I became a Registered Investment Advisor, with no ties to proprietary products, sales quotas or company priorities. Because I'm not limited by membership with a specific Broker-Dealer, or a captive agent to "commission only" products, I can offer a wider selection of financial products and providers to better match client needs. My mission is to find the most effective products, which best satisfy the interests of my clients! There are no outside interests standing between me and my clients. I want to be a partner in their financial futures. There are better things available!
