Default thumbnails

By default, Bootstrap's thumbnails are designed to showcase linked images with minimal required markup. And with a bit of extra markup, it's possible to add any kind of HTML content like headings, paragraphs, or buttons into thumbnails. This was originally modeled on Bootstrap v2.3.2, but updated slightly with some v3.1.1 updating.
