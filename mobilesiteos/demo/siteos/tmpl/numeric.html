Numeric Input Control

The below is a demo of our "Numeric control" widget for input:text inputs. This page is used for debugging of the msos.number_ctrl module, which we use in conjunction with our msos.html5.numeric module. Together they replace input:number inputs in non-compliant HTML5 browsers with a input:text field and numeric control widget.
