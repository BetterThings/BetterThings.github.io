Financial Planning is Best Done Before You Need It

"A good financial plan is a road map that shows us exactly how the choices we make today will affect our future" - Alexa Von Tobel

Comprehensive Planning

Better Things Financial Partners offers financial planning services tailored to meet the specific and changing needs of our clients. Properly done, financial planning is the process that gives people control over their financial circumstances by:

  • Clearly identifying where they are today. This includes an objective assessment of strengths and opportunities as well as challenges
  • Defining and prioritizing what they want or need to accomplish with their wealth along with a timeline for those priorities
  • Developing an achievable strategy to attain objectives
  • Monitoring progress while ensuring flexibility and options

Areas of Planning

We want to ensure that all areas of your financial life are working within a cohesive strategy targeted towards your financial goals. We partner with our clients to plot a course for financial success in the following areas:

  • Retirement Planning
  • Education Funding
  • Cash Flow Management
  • Insurance Planning
  • Budgeting & Debt Management
  • Investment Planning
  • Estate Planning
  • Business Planning

How we can help

Whether you are building assets for the future or seeking to protect, enjoy and pass on your assets to the next generation, we can help you if your objectives include:

  • Building retirement income
  • Creation and preservation of personal wealth
  • Reducing the risk of financially devastating events
  • Maximizing wealth transfer to your heirs
  • Minimizing the hazards of inflation
  • Adding perspective and discipline to your financial life

Our client-centered focus begins with a complimentary meeting to determine if we are a good fit for each other by reviewing your concerns, goals and requirements.

Unlike some other financial planning firms, we realize that people from all walks of life and any age can benefit from the peace of mind achieved through financial planning. Therefore, our clients choose the level of service appropriate for their needs and budgets.

If you have a nagging uneasiness about your current position or the future, you will be surprised how much comfort and peace there is in having a seasoned and unbiased professional work together with you to help you make sure your goals are more than wishes.
