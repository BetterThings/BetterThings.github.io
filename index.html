A Better Advisor Relationship


Chip Estes, President

Better Things Financial Partners, LLC (BTFP) is a Registered Investment Advisor (RIA). We must follow the highest known standard in law, known as the “trust” standard. This means that we are required by law to place the interests of our clients before our own, and fulfill critical fiduciary duties in trust and confidence. Why is this important? Here are some of the benefits:

  • Better Fiduciary with a Registered Investment Advisor
  • Client centric, not product centric counseling
  • Maintain a trust fiduciary responsibility to our clients

In other words, we must provide our “best impartial advice”.

Registered Investment Advisor vs. Broker-Dealers

By contrast, broker-dealers do not owe a fiduciary duty to their investment clients, but instead are required by federal law to act in the best interest of their employer instead. Brokers only have a to meet a "suitability" standard. This means they only need to provide suitable advice, which may or may not be the absolute best advice for the client.

At BTFP, we do not work for a broker-dealer: we work only for you, our client. Have a look at this video, linked here, which illustrates the difference between a "fiduciary" versus "suitability" standard of client advisement.
